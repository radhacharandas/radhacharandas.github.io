About Me

Hi, I am Radhacharan Das. I am an aspiring dasi of Hit Sajani. I love watching nature documentaries, adore cooking and enjoy long solitary walks in the park! I also love cats and anything remotely related to them. In my free time, I cook, read and translate! I consider myself a good artist too!

Web Development

Cooking

I enjoy cooking a wide variety of food! I love incorporating chickpeas and guacamole to my diet.
I only cook for the pleasure of Sri Sri Hit Harivansh ju and my most beloved Radha Vallabh.

Seva

My guru is Sri Hit Premananda Maharaj. I am doing ashtayam seva under his merciful guidance.
I enjoy singing padas and offering arati. I love dressing up my Thakurjis and doing flower seva.
